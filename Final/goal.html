Quality & Sustainability

Every item is made from scratch. This includes the cutting, distressing and sewing.


Our goal at FreeWayStudios is to provide unique, 1 of 1 clothing in the most sustaineable fashion. We source our fabrics from donated clothing, thrift shops, or sustaineable sections at fabric stores to ensure we do not contribute to fast fashion or to further damaging of our beautiful planet. Every item is hand cut, distressed, and sewn, ensuring no other garment will be like the one you've purchased.
